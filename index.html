Free MTG Playtester — Play Magic: The Gathering Online Without Downloading Anything

TCG Playsim is a free, browser-based playtester for Magic: The Gathering. Play MTG online for free with up to four players in real-time multiplayer rooms. No download, no install, no account required to get started.

Supported Formats

Build and play decks in Standard, Commander (EDH), Oathbreaker, Modern, Pioneer, Pauper, Legacy, and the shared-deck Dandân format. Each format is supported in the deckbuilder with appropriate validation.

A Free MTG Arena and MTGO Alternative

If you are looking for a free MTG Arena alternative or a way to play MTGO in your browser without paying for digital cards, TCG Playsim is a manual playtester that runs in any modern browser. Every card from Scryfall is available — you don't grind a collection, you just build and play.

Commander, EDH, and Oathbreaker

Commander (EDH) is fully supported with command-zone tracking, partner commanders, and 2-4 player free-for-all rooms. Oathbreaker decks track the planeswalker and signature spell separately.

Dandân Format

TCG Playsim ships with several Dandân format variants ready to play. Both players draw from a single shared 60-card library — the format is built around the card Dandân and other island-walking creatures. Pick a variant in the deckbuilder.
