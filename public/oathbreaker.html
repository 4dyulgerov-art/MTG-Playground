What is Oathbreaker?

Oathbreaker is a 60-card singleton format with a planeswalker as your "oathbreaker" (commander equivalent) plus a signature spell — an instant or sorcery that can only be cast with the planeswalker on the battlefield. Both start in the command zone. Decks are singleton; colour identity matches the planeswalker. Starting life total is 20 (vs. Commander's 40), making games shorter and the planeswalker's loyalty matter more.

The format is small but devoted. There aren't many places to play it online — most clients treat it as a Commander variant with manual setup, which is fine but inelegant.

Building an Oathbreaker deck

New Deck → format: Oathbreaker → set planeswalker → set signature spell → build the 58 other cards. Colour identity is the planeswalker's. Singleton rules apply (one of each non-basic). Most decks lean into either tutoring up the signature spell, protecting the oathbreaker, or both.

Multiplayer Oathbreaker

The format plays cleanly in 2 to 4 player rooms. Each player has their own oathbreaker + signature spell. Free-for-all is the default; politics is part of the meta.
